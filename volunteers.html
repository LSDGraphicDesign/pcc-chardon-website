Elders

Our elders provide spiritual leadership, serve Communion, and support the pastor in worship and care ministries. They offer wisdom, guidance, and prayerful presence during services and throughout the life of the church.

Deacons

Deacons serve by preparing and distributing Communion, greeting members and guests, and helping with worship logistics. They ensure our worship is welcoming, reverent, and deeply rooted in service.
