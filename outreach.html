Scout Troop Sponsor

Pilgrim Christian Church is proud to sponsor a local Boy Scout troop, providing a supportive space for leadership, service, and community-building through scouting values.

Alcoholics Anonymous

Our building hosts regular AA meetings, offering a safe and welcoming space for those seeking support and recovery. All are welcome and confidentiality is respected.

Pancake Breakfast

Each spring, PCC hosts a community Pancake Breakfast during the annual Maple Festival in Chardon. It's a joyful tradition that brings neighbors together over a warm meal.

Chili Dinner

Our annual Chili Dinner provides a hearty meal and warm fellowship on the night of the city’s tree lighting ceremony — a perfect way to kick off the holiday season.

Rummage Sale

The PCC Rummage Sale gives new life to gently used items while raising funds for ministries. It's a fun and eco-friendly way to serve the community and reduce waste.
