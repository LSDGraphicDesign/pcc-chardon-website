Scout Troop Sponsor

PCC proudly supports youth in our community as a sponsor for a local scout troop. We provide meeting space and encourage leadership and character development.

Alcoholics Anonymous

Alcoholics Anonymous holds meetings at PCC each week, offering support and healing in a safe, welcoming environment.

Pancake Breakfast

Held during Chardon’s annual Maple Festival, our pancake breakfast provides food, fellowship, and fun for all who attend. All are welcome to enjoy the community spirit!

Chili Dinner

Our chili dinner takes place during the city’s tree lighting ceremony, offering warmth and hospitality as the holiday season begins.

Rummage Sale

PCC hosts a seasonal rummage sale featuring donated goods, clothing, and home items. Proceeds support the church’s outreach work and missions.
