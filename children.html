Youth Group

Our Youth Group creates a fun and welcoming space for young people to grow in faith, build friendships, and serve their community. Activities include discussions, service projects, outings, and seasonal events.

Sunday School

Our Sunday School program nurtures children's faith with age-appropriate lessons, crafts, music, and stories from the Bible. Classes run during worship for kids of all ages, taught by dedicated volunteers.
