Polka Sunday

Polka Sunday is a special service held each summer at Big Creek Park, with joyful music provided by the world-famous Chardon Polka Band. Join us for worship outdoors with dancing, music, and laughter!

Mountain Run Station Worship

Enjoy a relaxed outdoor worship experience at the scenic Mountain Run Station. This service brings together nature, scripture, and community.

Church Picnic

Our church picnic features great food, fellowship, and games for all ages. It’s a time to celebrate summer and deepen our church family bonds.
