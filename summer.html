Polka Sunday

Polka Sunday is a joyful worship celebration featuring live polka music, energetic worship, and community fellowship. A tradition that brings fun and faith together in harmony!

Mountain Run Station Worship

We gather outdoors at Mountain Run Station for a special summer worship service surrounded by nature. This relaxed, spirit-filled gathering is open to all.

Church Picnic

Our summer church picnic brings families and friends together for food, games, and fellowship. It’s a time to relax, reconnect, and enjoy God’s blessings in community.

Ice Cream Social

End the summer with sweetness at our ice cream social! Enjoy scoops, toppings, and time together with your church family and neighbors.
