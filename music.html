Organ

Because of the deteriorating condition of our 1910 Moller organ, the decision to purchase a new pipe organ was made by the PCC Congregation in October of 1991. After a thorough study by our organ committee, the Holtkamp Organ Company was chosen to design our organ. Since 1855, this company has been building pipe organs in Cleveland under the leadership of the Holtkamp family. Pilgrim Christian Church is incredibly proud to have a Holtkamp Organ. Visit them here: http://www.holtkamporgan.com/
