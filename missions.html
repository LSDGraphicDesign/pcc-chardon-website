Heifer Fair

Each year we raise funds through our Heifer Fair to support Heifer International, helping to provide animals, training, and resources to communities in need around the world.

Crop Walk

PCC participates in the CROP Hunger Walk, raising awareness and funds to fight hunger locally and globally. Join our team and help us take steps toward a more food-secure future.

Project Hope Monthly Meal

Every month, we prepare and serve meals for those in need through Project Hope. Whether it's sandwiches, fresh produce, or a warm smile, our volunteers ensure no one goes hungry. Our kitchen becomes a place of love, laughter, and service for all involved.
