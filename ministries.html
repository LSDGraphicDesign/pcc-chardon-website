Peace Crane Ministry

Rooted in the story of Sadako Sasaki, a young girl impacted by the Hiroshima bombing, peace cranes have come to symbolize hope, healing, and nonviolence. Today, our Peace Crane Ministry invites people of all ages to fold and share paper cranes as acts of prayer and solidarity, spreading messages of compassion across communities and even around the world.

Ida's Pantry

PCC's food pantry provides groceries to families in need. As the second-largest food cupboard in Geauga County, we proudly serve our neighbors with dignity and compassion. No one should go hungry in our community.

Pantry Hours:
Monday - Thursday: 10:30 AM – 11:30 AM
Friday: 3:30 PM – 4:30 PM

Project Hope Monthly Meal

Every month, we prepare and serve meals for those in need through Project Hope. Whether it's sandwiches, fresh produce, or a warm smile, our volunteers ensure no one goes hungry. Our kitchen becomes a place of love, laughter, and service for all involved.
